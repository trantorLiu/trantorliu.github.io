As the founder of CakeResume, I envisioned a system that could help to solve the challenges I encountered while trying to write a resume that accurately reflected my experience. CakeResume  is a highly customized creative resume building program. The drag-and-drop resume editor comes with 50+ resume snippets, so that users can quickly build a unique and vivid resume.
 As the solo founder of CakeResume I am currently seeking co-founders. I would love to hear from you if you're interested in joining this venture!

Experiences

CakeResume, Founder, Mar 2015 - Present

As the technical founder, I developed the website on my own. I worked with freelance writers, social media managers, and designers to build the product.

Codementor, Full-Stack Web Developer, Oct 2014 - Mar 2016

Codementor is a live 1:1 help marketplace for developers, making it easy for developers to get instant help from experts via code/screen sharing, video, and text.
I helped the company build Codementor Monthly, Codementor Live Classes, and a data tracking system that kept track of conversion rates.

Republic of China Armed Forces, Second Lieutenant, Aug 2013 - Jul 2014

As a second lieutenant chief counselor in the Republic of China Armed Forces, I built ArmyApp with 4 of my college friends. It helps soldiers report their locations during their vacation, & takes only 3 seconds to report. It caused an army-wide sensation and became news in Taiwan. Thus, the Chief of the Republic of China Army gave me a prize. The Army Command Headquarters also gave me a chance to lead a short term project to digitize other information for the army.
